{{#_}}City of Greensboro
Participatory Budgeting{{/_}}

{{#_}}Greensboro Participatory Budgeting is a democratic process in which community members from all five City Council districts are directly deciding how to spend $500,000 ($100,000 per district).{{/_}}

{{#_}}Idea submission for the PB process is now open!
If you have an idea for a project to improve your district, please share it on the map!{{/_}}
{{#_}}
From December 2015 to March 2016, volunteer budget delegates will work to develop these initial ideas into full project proposals. Greensboro residents will able to vote from April-May 2016 to determine the winning projects. {{/_}}

{{#_}}For more information on participatory budgeting in Greensboro, visit greensboro-nc.gov.{{/_}}

{{#_}}For information on the nationwide movement for PB, visit the website of the Participatory Budgeting Project{{/_}}.
